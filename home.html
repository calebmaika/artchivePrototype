Sharing Chapter 3 of my ongoing serial novel "Whispers in the Dark". This chapter introduces the mysterious antagonist. Would love your thoughts on the character development!

Chapter 3: The Shadow

Detective Clara Moore stood in the abandoned warehouse, her breath visible in the cold air. The note had led her here—another cryptic message in the same elegant script. "You're getting warmer, detective. But can you handle the truth when it burns?"

Her flashlight beam cut through the darkness, revealing strange symbols painted on the walls. They matched the ones from the cold case files back at the station—the ones marked "unsolved." A chill ran down her spine that had nothing to do with the winter air.

Then she heard it—a whisper so faint she thought she imagined it. But it came again, clearer this time: "Clara... you should have stopped looking."

She spun around, her service weapon drawn, but the warehouse was empty. Except for one thing—a single playing card placed carefully on the floor. The queen of hearts, with a drop of what looked like blood staining the corner.
