Chapter 1: The Awakening

The city slept under a blanket of stars, unaware of the storm that approached. Elias stood at the edge of the rooftop, his coat flapping in the wind like the wings of a trapped bird. Below him, the streets pulsed with the rhythm of late-night revelers, their laughter rising like bubbles in champagne.

He had come here to think, to escape the noise of his own mind, but the height only made his thoughts more dizzying. The decision weighed on him like an anchor tied to his soul. To jump would be to surrender, to step back would be to continue the fight - but what was the fight even for anymore?
